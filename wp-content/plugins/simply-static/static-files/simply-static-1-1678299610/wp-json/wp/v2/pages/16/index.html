Our Journey<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Starting out on 7th July 2016, we were a single door outpatient clinic providing general medical consultation, basic laboratory services and dispensary. Two months later, we introduced a four bed capacity in-patient department which has now grown into a twenty eight bed capacity department with three categories of wards in three years.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We transformed our laboratory services from preforming basic laboratory tests to a level II Laboratory with advanced test profile. We also pioneered adult immunization to the general population that we later scaled to include Anti Rabies vaccine for animal bites, Hepatitis B, Vaccination, Typhoid Vaccine and HPV vaccination.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our staff has grown from four people that included two doctors, one laboratory assistant and one nurse, to 43 health care professionals that provide dedicated health service delivery to our clientele every day.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
